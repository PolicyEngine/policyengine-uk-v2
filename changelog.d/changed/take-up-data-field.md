Benefit take-up is now a persisted benunit data field (`claims_uc_if_eligible`) rather than a load-time flag. It governs take-up of every means-tested benefit (UC, HB, CTC, WTC, IS, ESA, JSA) and defaults to true, so hypothetical households claim everything they're eligible for. The `full_take_up` parameter on `Simulation.__init__` and the `--full-take-up` CLI flag are removed; survey records carry the field set to their reported claim status. Existing clean datasets without the column fall back to `on_uc`, so they behave exactly as before.
