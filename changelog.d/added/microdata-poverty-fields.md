Household microdata now carries after-housing-costs income (`net_income_ahc`, `equivalised_net_income_ahc`) and per-household poverty flags (`in_relative_poverty_bhc`/`_ahc`, `in_absolute_poverty_bhc`/`_ahc`) for both baseline and reform. Relative lines are 60% of the baseline person-weighted median equivalised income; absolute lines uprate the 2010/11 reference by CPI — matching the aggregate headcounts in `run::analyse`.
