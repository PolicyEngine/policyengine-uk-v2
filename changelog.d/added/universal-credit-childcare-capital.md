Expand the Universal Credit calculation into a fuller programme structure, adding two elements that were previously missing. The childcare element (`uc_childcare_element_monthly`) reimburses 85% of registered childcare costs for working benunits, capped at the DWP 2025/26 monthly maxima (£1,031.88 for one child, £1,768.94 for two or more; UC Regs 2013 regs.31–35). The capital test (`uc_tariff_income_annual` plus a cliff in `calculate_universal_credit`) disregards capital below £6,000, treats capital of £16,000 or more as extinguishing entitlement, and accrues £4.35/month of tariff income per £250 of capital between the limits (reg.18, reg.72). New `UniversalCreditParams` fields are loaded with serde defaults so prior-year YAMLs continue to deserialise, and 2025/26 values are set in `parameters/2025_26.yaml`. Worked-example unit tests cover a couple with two children, the earnings taper, the £16k capital cliff, tariff income, and the childcare element.
