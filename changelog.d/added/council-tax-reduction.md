Add Council Tax Reduction / Council Tax Benefit (CTR/CTB), the means-tested support that reduces a household's council tax liability (Local Government Finance Act 2012 s.10; Council Tax Reduction Schemes (England) Regulations 2012, SI 2012/2885). New `src/variables/council_tax.rs` module and `CouncilTaxReductionParams` (20% taper per SI 2012/2885 Sch.1 para.30; pension-age claimants get up to 100% support, working-age capped at a representative England-average 90% via `max_support_working_age`). The means test mirrors Housing Benefit (weekly applicable amount vs income, excess tapered) and is applied to the household reference person's benefit unit against the calculated council tax. CTR is surfaced on `HouseholdResult.council_tax_reduction`, counted in `total_benefits`, and added to HBAI net income — flowing through consistently with other benefits. 2025/26 parameters set in `parameters/2025_26.yaml`. Council tax liability itself (band × LA-multiplier with the single-person discount) was already modelled in `wealth_taxes`. Scotland/Wales devolved CTR variants are documented in `LEGISLATIVE_REFERENCE.md` as remaining work. Addresses #42.
