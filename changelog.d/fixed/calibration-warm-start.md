Removed spurious year-on-year decile income volatility (a sawtooth, worst in forecast years) by stabilising weight calibration. Each EFRS year now warm-starts the optimiser from the previous year's calibrated weights, matched by a cross-year provenance key, so the solver no longer wanders the decile null space and lands on a different composition each year. Separately, sub-£50m sum targets and sub-10k-people count targets are now dropped from the loss so micro-targets the survey can't represent (transitional UC in its 2013 pilot, tiny dividend bands) no longer dominate the magnitude-blind RMSRE.
